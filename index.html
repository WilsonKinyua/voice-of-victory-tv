Living in Christ

Your Walk With The Spirit

Your whole life ought to be based on nothing else but your walk with the Holy Spirit; that’s the secret to a life of absolute and unlimited success. When you spend quality time in fellowship with the Holy Spirit, He lifts your vision and enables you to see like God.

Understanding Prayer in the New Testament

For the New Testament believer, prayer is not just a means of religious communication with God; it’s a fellowship. Some people pray because they’ve been brought up to think it’s a religious obligation they must fulfill.

Expect Answers When You Pray

God invites us to pray; He’s not troubled with our prayers. Nothing is impossible with Him. When you pray, God listens because you’re His child, and you pray according to His will. This is God’s Word to you: If you ask Him, you shall receive! Amen
